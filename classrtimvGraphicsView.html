◆ centerOn()

void rtimvGraphicsView::centerOn ( qreal  x,
qreal  y 
)

Center the view port on the given scene coordinate.

Overloads QGraphicsView::centerOn so we can capture this coordinate for later use. After setting m_xCen=x and m_yCen = y, just calls QGraphicsView::centerOn(x,y).

Parameters
[in]xThe scene x coordinate on which to center
[in]yThe scene y coordinate on which to center
